Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a warranty if things don't work out?

Yes! Every match comes with a 30-day wellness warranty. If your new feathered friend isn't adjusting well or you have unexpected concerns, we'll work with you to ensure the best outcome—whether that means extra support, a health check, or helping you find a better match. Your peace of mind (and your parrot's happiness) matters to us.

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our algorithm learns your preferences as you swipe—tracking which birds you like and dislike. It looks for patterns in your choices and compares them with others who have similar tastes. Over time, it uses this information to recommend birds you're more likely to match with, making your experience more personalized with every swipe.

Is it easy to own a parrot?

Owning a parrot can be incredibly rewarding, but it does take commitment. Parrots are intelligent, social creatures that need daily interaction, mental stimulation, and proper care to thrive. While some are low-maintenance, many require time, patience, and a good understanding of their needs. With the right environment and attention, owning a parrot can be a joyful and lasting companionship.

Are there local vets who understand my parrot's needs?

Absolutely! We partner with a network of trusted avian specialists—like Dr. Featherstone at Blue Ridge Bird Care and the team at Squawk & Heal Clinic —who are familiar with the unique needs of parrots. Whether it's a routine checkup or specialized advice, our local partners are here to help keep your feathered friend happy and healthy.

Is there a fee to use Soothing Squawks?

The basic service is free, and you can enjoy browsing and interacting with bird profiles at no cost. We do offer premium features for advanced matching and additional functionality.
